A food and drink guide near campus

Introduction

Welcome to my food blog where I post about the restaurants I have visited and subsequently the meals I found the most delicious.

For today's post I will be focusing on several locations found around the Petty Building of UNCG, specifically places within a half mile.

Our three locations today will feature a pizza place and a seperate wing place to match, along with a pita place to add variaty.

Please feel free to visit any of these places and try any of the meals featured here or branch out on your own.

Restaurant Showcases

Slices Pizza Co.


Slices pizza co. is a wonderful pizza shop whose main feature lay in selling pizza's by the slice. While there are other options such as pastas and deserts I definitely recommend digging in with some of their very generous slices of pizza.


Baked Zitti

Classic Italian Baked Zitti with generous portions of sauce and cheese.


BBQ Chicken Pizza

Delicious twist of a normal pizza featuring a wonderful combination of BBQ sauce and chicken.


Cheese Pizza

Back to the regular cheese pizza, a time honored classic still near and dear to many hearts.

East Coast Wings


East Coast Wings is a popular restaurant chain providing a relaxing family friendly place to eat while remaining true to their roots as a wing/bar place. Their sauced wings both bone in and boneless are exceptional along with the quality and speed of their service.


Boneless Wings

The go to wing for many who visit, Perfectly cooked wings slathered in whichever of their sauces suite you.


Bone-in Wings

For those who enjoy eating their chicken right off the bone, flavorful and coated however spicy you can order.


Bacon Jam Burger

A slight twist from the typical wings featured. Every place of this image has to have a good burger to match.

Pita Delite


Pita Delite is a new personal discovery of mine as I had never been to a pita place before. The experience has been very new and unique for me and while I cant yet speak for everything on the menu the bowls of kabobs have been wonderful.


Bowls

One of my favorites, a base of pasta saled or rice, meat of your choice, and several wonderful sauces.


Gyro

The most well known and classic of greek foods, packed to the brim with food and worth every dime.


Chicken Kabob

A more modest and approchable meal with solid slices of chicken and sides to pair perfectly with.

Extra Info

Slices and East Coast Wings are both located just slightly off campus and only accept typical forms of payment while Pita Delite is located on campus and can accept flex cash.

While visiting Pita Delite feel free to stop by and try buffalo wild wings and salsaritas as well.
